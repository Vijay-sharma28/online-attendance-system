Attendance Management System

ABOUT

Our Online Attendance System is designed to simplify and modernize the way attendance is managed in college. By replacing traditional paper-based methods with a digital solution, we aim to save time, reduce errors, and provide real-time tracking of student attendance.

This system allows faculty to mark attendance with just a few clicks. Admins can generate detailed reports and analytics to monitor overall participation and performance.

With a user-friendly interface and secure login system, our platform ensures convenience, accuracy, and data security for everyone involved.
